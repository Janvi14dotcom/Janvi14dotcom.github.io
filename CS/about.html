About Infant Jesus School Patna

For God and Country - Building foundations for a brighter future

Our Mission

Welcome to Infant Jesus School, the esteemed institution where the transformative journey of learning, values, and success unfolds. At Infant Jesus School, we are committed to providing quality education that nurtures the intellectual, emotional, and spiritual growth of every child. Our e-learning platform extends our mission beyond the classroom, making education accessible and engaging for all students.

Our Vision

Inspired by our motto, 'For God and Country,' we are committed to nurturing not just academic prowess but also instilling values that shape responsible citizens. At Infant Jesus School, education transcends textbooks; it's about fostering individuals who embody integrity, compassion, and a sense of social responsibility.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We maintain the highest standards in education
  • Compassion: We care for each child with love and understanding
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty and moral principles
  • Innovation: We embrace modern teaching methods
  • Community: We foster a supportive learning environment

Our History

Established in 1978 by the visionary Anne Joseph, our school stands as a testament to a legacy built on unwavering dedication to academic excellence and character development. From our modest beginnings in a rented premise, supported by the generosity of Anne's parents, we have flourished into a premier secondary school renowned for its holistic approach to education. Infant Jesus School has been a pillar of educational excellence for over four decades, serving countless students and families in Patna.
